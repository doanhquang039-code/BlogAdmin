6. 🖱️ **Hover Effects** - Cards scale and glow
7. 📱 **Smooth Scroll** - Offset for navbar
8. 🎯 **Active Nav** - Highlights current section
9. ⬆️ **Scroll to Top** - Fades in/out
10. ⏱️ **Timeline Dots** - Scale on hover
